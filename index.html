About Me

I'm a dedicated geologist with a strong foundation in GIS technologies and field survey techniques. My journey in geology began at AVS College of Arts & Sciences, where I earned my B.Sc in Geology with 72%, and continued with an M.Sc from Annamalai University with a CGPA of 7.3, specializing in geological mapping and petrology.

Currently serving as a Site Supervisor at Velciti Consulting Engineers Pvt Ltd, I oversee multiple renewable energy projects including wind and solar installations for major clients like Jindal Steel Power (350 MW Wind Power Project), Ecoren, and Hero Future Energies across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu.

My expertise lies in bridging traditional geological fieldwork with cutting-edge GIS technologies such as QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, LIDAR, and ArcPy to deliver comprehensive geological assessments and actionable insights for sustainable energy development. I'm proficient in multiple languages including English, Tamil, and Kannada, which helps me coordinate effectively with diverse teams.
